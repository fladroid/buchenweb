Language Learning

Choose a game and start learning languages from classic literature.

✏️

Fill in the Blank

Read a sentence from classic literature and fill in the missing words. Learn a foreign language with English as context, or practise English with a translation as hint.

  • Easy: 1 blank · Medium: 2 · Hard: 3
  • Click blank to pick from multiple choice, then type if wrong
  • Hint reveals one word (−3 pts) · 10 sentences per round
🔗

Sentence Match

Two columns — English on the left, translation on the right. Click one sentence from each side to pair them. Train your eye to recognise which translation belongs to which original.

  • 10 sentence pairs per round
  • +10 pts for a correct match, −2 pts for a wrong pair
  • Match all pairs as quickly as possible
🃏

Memory

Classic card-flip game. Flip two cards at a time and find matching English–translation pairs. Cards stay face up when matched — fewer attempts means a better score.

  • 5 pairs = 10 cards on the board
  • Find all pairs in as few attempts as possible
  • Unmatched cards flip back after a short delay
🔀

Scrambled Sentence

The words of a translated sentence are shuffled. Click them in the right order to reconstruct the translation. The English sentence is shown as context throughout.

  • 5 sentences per round
  • +10 pts first try, +5 pts after rearranging, 0 pts if wrong
  • Wrong answer reveals the correct sentence
